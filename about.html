Our Story

Founded in 1999 by Dr. Elizabeth MedCare and a group of visionary healthcare professionals, MedCare Hospital began as a small community clinic with a big dream: to provide world-class healthcare that's accessible, affordable, and centered around patient needs.

Over the past 25 years, we've grown from a 20-bed facility to a comprehensive medical center with over 200 beds, state-of-the-art equipment, and a team of more than 150 specialists. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, from being the first hospital in the region to implement electronic health records to pioneering minimally invasive surgical techniques.

Today, MedCare Hospital stands as a beacon of hope and healing in our community, serving over 50,000 patients annually while maintaining the personal touch and compassionate care that has defined us from the beginning.
