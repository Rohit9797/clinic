Appointments

You can schedule an appointment in several ways:

  • Online: Use our online appointment booking system available 24/7
  • Phone: Call our main number at +1-555-MEDCARE (Mon-Fri 8AM-6PM)
  • In Person: Visit our registration desk at the main campus

For urgent appointments, please call during business hours or visit our emergency department for immediate care.

You can schedule appointments up to 6 months in advance. However, availability varies by department and doctor:

  • Primary Care: Usually available within 1-2 weeks
  • Specialists: May require 2-4 weeks advance booking
  • Procedures: Scheduling depends on complexity and preparation requirements

We recommend booking as early as possible to secure your preferred time slot.

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule appointments with at least 24 hours notice:

  • Online: Log into your patient portal to manage appointments
  • Phone: Call +1-555-MEDCARE during business hours
  • Cancellation Policy: Less than 24 hours notice may result in a cancellation fee

Emergency situations are always accommodated without penalty.

Please bring the following items to your appointment:

  • Valid photo ID (driver's license, passport, etc.)
  • Insurance card and any referral forms
  • List of current medications and dosages
  • Previous medical records or test results (if applicable)
  • Payment method for copays or deductibles
  • List of questions or concerns you want to discuss

Insurance & Billing

We accept most major insurance plans, including:

  • BlueCross BlueShield
  • Aetna
  • Humana
  • Cigna
  • Medicare and Medicare Advantage plans
  • Medicaid
  • Most employer-sponsored health plans

Please contact our billing department at +1-555-BILLING to verify your specific plan coverage.

We offer several convenient payment options:

  • Online: Pay through our secure patient portal
  • Phone: Call our billing department at +1-555-BILLING
  • Mail: Send payment to the address on your bill
  • In Person: Visit our billing office during business hours
  • Auto-Pay: Set up automatic payments for convenience

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. Payment plans are available for qualifying patients.

Yes, we offer several financial assistance programs:

  • Charity Care: Free or reduced-cost care for qualifying patients
  • Payment Plans: Interest-free payment arrangements
  • Sliding Scale: Fees based on income and family size
  • Medicaid Assistance: Help with Medicaid applications

Contact our financial counselors at +1-555-BILLING to discuss your options and apply for assistance.

Medical Services

MedCare Hospital offers comprehensive medical services including:

  • Emergency Care: 24/7 emergency department
  • Cardiology: Heart and vascular care
  • Pediatrics: Specialized care for children
  • Orthopedics: Bone, joint, and muscle care
  • Neurology: Brain and nervous system care
  • Oncology: Cancer treatment and care
  • Women's Health: Obstetrics and gynecology
  • Diagnostic Services: Lab, imaging, and testing

Visit our Services page for detailed information about each department.

Referral requirements depend on your insurance plan:

  • HMO Plans: Usually require a referral from your primary care physician
  • PPO Plans: Typically allow direct access to specialists
  • Medicare: Most services don't require referrals
  • Self-Pay: No referral needed

We recommend checking with your insurance provider or calling our scheduling team to confirm requirements for your specific plan.

Test results are available through multiple channels:

  • Patient Portal: Secure online access to results (recommended)
  • Phone: Call your doctor's office for results discussion
  • Follow-up Appointment: Review results with your physician
  • Mail: Physical copies can be mailed upon request

Most routine lab results are available within 24-48 hours. Complex tests may take longer. Your doctor will contact you directly for any urgent or abnormal results.

Patient Care

Our visiting hours are designed to balance patient rest with family support:

  • General Units: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM daily
  • ICU: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Pediatrics: Parents/guardians may stay 24/7
  • Maternity: Partners may stay overnight
  • Emergency Department: Limited to immediate family

Visitor restrictions may apply during flu season or other health emergencies. Please check current policies when planning your visit.

Yes, we offer convenient parking options:

  • Patient Parking: Free for patients and visitors
  • Valet Service: Available Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Accessible Parking: Reserved spaces near all entrances
  • Emergency Parking: Designated spots near the emergency entrance
  • Overnight Parking: Available for extended stays

Parking validation is provided at the information desk for visits over 2 hours.

We provide various amenities to ensure comfort during your stay:

  • Cafeteria: Fresh meals and snacks available daily
  • Gift Shop: Flowers, cards, and personal items
  • Chapel: Quiet space for prayer and reflection
  • Family Lounges: Comfortable waiting areas with WiFi
  • ATM: Available in the main lobby
  • Pharmacy: On-site prescription services
  • WiFi: Free wireless internet throughout the facility

We value your feedback and offer several ways to share your experience:

  • Patient Satisfaction Survey: Mailed after discharge
  • Online Feedback: Submit comments through our website
  • Patient Relations: Call +1-555-FEEDBACK
  • Suggestion Boxes: Located throughout the hospital
  • Direct Communication: Speak with your nurse manager

All feedback is reviewed by our quality improvement team to enhance patient care and services.
